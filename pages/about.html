You can call me Dan ;)

I love travelling, learning new things and making new friends...

Asia always seemed a world away. I'm originally from Toronto, and after years working at a miserable job, I realized it is never too late to explore. I thought to myself, with the money I had been spending on rent, living somewhere I didn't really want to be in, perhaps I could use these resources elsewhere? So decided to take the plunge into teaching by taking a CELTA course to teach English as a second language. I then applied to teach in South Korea, and I was fortunate to land a position with the Ministry of Education. I was placed in a countryside town in the Chungbuk province, where I would divide my work week between two elementary schools and one middle school. And so my adventure began...

After three years in Korea, I was ready for the next challenge. And this brings me to Berlin. A friend in the countryside town I was living in, had suggested to me to apply for a master's program in Germany. So once again, I took a plunge and applied to HWR Berlin, and to my surprise, I was accepted to a program in International Security Management. And so the adventure goes on...
